Consent Details

Service Overview

You are being offered 12 therapy sessions through The Children's Society. These sessions will:

  • Be 50 minutes long
  • Take place online via secure video call
  • Be scheduled at times that work for you
  • Be completely confidential

Data Protection

Your personal information will be handled securely and confidentially. We will:

  • Keep your information safe and private
  • Only share information if required by law
  • Delete unnecessary data when no longer needed
  • Give you access to your information upon request

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request your data be deleted
  • Access your therapy records
  • Choose how we contact you
